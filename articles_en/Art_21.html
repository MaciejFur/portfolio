Examples of Shaders and VFXs made in Unity:

Dissolve + Polar Coordinates

Dissolve with additional use of "polar coordinates", for spiral effect.

Made for: URP



Electric + Air Bubble

Shader made for - Cubeloop.
All effects including electricity were prepared using noise with a polar coordinates for a spiral look. Polar coordinates in this case are also used for erasing the UV borders (exlusively for electricity).

Made for: URP



Air Bubble

Shader made for - Cubeloop.
Effect accomplished by using three separate noise maps, moving in different directions and speed, which helps with achieving sharper shapes. Inspired by Julian Love presentation about VFX made for Diablo III.

Made for: URP



Alpha Bars

Cutout bars based on the scene view. One of the college exercises.

Made for: URP



Potion

Potion with a wobbly liquid.

Made for: URP



Toon Shader

Cell shading made for - Cubeloop made in HLSL.

Made for: URP



Custom Renderer

(for hidden objects)

Player mesh rendered behind the objects using URP Custom Renderer.

In addition to that, in the attached gif Toon Shader + Outline added directly to the Renderer for all objects in camera.

Made for Cubeloop (previous versions).
